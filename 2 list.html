List

Types of lists

  • ordered list
  • unordered list
  • description list

Ordered list

These lists will be having series of numbers infront of list.Eg:

    Popular animals

  1. cat
  2. dog
  3. cow
  4. goat

unordered list

These lists will be having bullets/squares/circles infront of list.Eg:

    Popular birds

  • crow
  • sparrow
  • dove
  • parrot
